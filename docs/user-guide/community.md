# Community & Groups

One of VeTerex's most powerful features is connecting you with others who share your achievements.

## The Power of Shared Experiences

When two people have both completed the same book, movie, or show, there's an instant connection. VeTerex makes these connections possible through verified, on-chain proof.

## Groups

### What are Groups?

Groups are communities of users who have all completed the same media. Each piece of media has its own group.

### Joining a Group

**Requirement:** You must own the completion NFT

1. Complete the media and mint your NFT
2. Go to the media detail page
3. Click "Join Group"
4. You're in!

VeTerex can identify users who share completions with you:

3. See users who have matching completions

## Privacy & Safety

### On-Chain Verification
- No one can fake completions
- Group membership is token-gated
- Authentic communities only

### Your Control
- Choose which groups to join
- Leave groups anytime
- Control your visibility

## Building Your Community

### Tips for Connection

1. **Complete popular media** - Larger communities
2. **Join groups actively** - Find your people
3. **Check similar users** - Discover new connections
4. **Be genuine** - The whole point is authenticity!
