## How It Works

### Step 1: Get Started
1. Download [VeryChat](https://www.verychat.io/) or use the VeTerex extension
2. Connect wallet (Google, Apple, or Discord login supported)
3. Enable media tracking

### Step 2: Consume Media
Watch, read, or listen on supported platforms:
- **Video**: YouTube, Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, Crunchyroll
- **Reading**: Goodreads, Kindle, MangaDex
- **Tracking Sites**: MyAnimeList, AniList

### Step 3: Get Verified
VeTerex automatically tracks progress and detects completions. Our backend verifies genuine completions before allowing mints.

### Step 4: Mint Your NFT
When you complete media, mint a soulbound NFT to your wallet - **completely free**.

### Step 5: Join Communities
Connect with other users who completed the same content. Chat, discover similar users, and build your verified network.

## How NFTs Are Minted

### The Process

1. **Completion Detected**
   - VeTerex extension monitors progress
   - Backend verifies genuine completion

2. **Mint Prompt**
   ```
   ğŸ‰ You completed "Attack on Titan"!
   [Mint NFT] [Dismiss]
   ```

3. **On-Chain Record Created**
   - NFT minted to your wallet on Verychain
   - Gas fees covered by VeTerex (FREE)
   - Token is soulbound (non-transferable)

### NFT Properties
- **Token ID**: Unique identifier
- **Media Type**: Book, Movie, Anime, etc.
- **Media Name**: Title of content
- **Timestamp**: When completed
- **Soulbound**: Cannot be transferred
