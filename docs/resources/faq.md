# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is VeTerex?
VeTerex is a decentralized platform for tracking media consumption and minting soulbound NFTs as proof of completion. It lets you verify your achievements and connect with others who share them.

### Is VeTerex free to use?
Yes! VeTerex is free to use. Minting NFTs is also free - we cover the gas fees.

### What media types are supported?
Books, Movies, Anime, Manga, Comics, and TV Shows.

---

## Wallet & Account

### Do I need a crypto wallet?
Yes, but VeTerex makes it easy. You can create a wallet with just your Google, Apple, or Discord account using our Wepin integration.

### What blockchain is VeTerex on?
VeTerex is built on **Verychain** (Chain ID: 4613), an EVM-compatible blockchain.

### Do I need to buy cryptocurrency?
No! Minting is free and VeTerex covers gas fees.

---

## NFTs

### What are soulbound NFTs?
Soulbound NFTs are non-transferable tokens permanently bound to your wallet. They cannot be sold or traded, ensuring authenticity.

### Can I sell my completion NFTs?
No. VeTerex NFTs are soulbound and cannot be transferred. This is by design to ensure achievements are authentic.

### Can I delete an NFT?
Yes, you can "burn" (permanently delete) any NFT you own. This cannot be undone.

### How do I verify my completion?
VeTerex uses automatic tracking for supported platforms. Our backend verifies completions before allowing mints.

---

## Tracking

### How does tracking work?
The VeTerex Chrome extension monitors your viewing/reading progress on supported platforms and detects when you complete media.

### Is my browsing tracked?
No. VeTerex only tracks activity on supported platforms when you have tracking enabled. General browsing is not monitored.

### What platforms are supported?
YouTube, Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, Crunchyroll, Goodreads, Kindle, MangaDex, MyAnimeList, and AniList.

### Can I disable tracking?
Yes, you can toggle tracking on/off anytime in Settings.

## Community

### How do group chats work?
Groups are created for each piece of media. Only users who have completed (and minted) that media can join the group.

### How do I find similar users?
Go to "Find Similar Users" and select NFTs from your collection. VeTerex will show other users with matching completions.

### Is messaging free?
Yes, messaging through VeryChat integration is free.
